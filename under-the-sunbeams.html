Under the sunbeams/shadows

By Jolyon Joyce (All Rights Reserved). Written in August 2022 while travelling in Italy.

Under the sunbeams by morning,
the heart's songs go wandering free;
shadows are missing faces, unconcious
of fading to the stars, gone... Unyielding
evening sighs, burdened by the wise.

Under the shadows of evening,
the heart's songs are fading to sighs;
sunbeams go missing. The stars, burdened
by wandering faces, gone by the
morning free, unconscious, unyielding: wise.
